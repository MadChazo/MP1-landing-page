About Us

ACME Corp. is an organization devoted to restoring world elephant populations and helping increase scientific understanding of these magnificent animals.

Elephant Facts

  • Elephants are the largest living land animals.
  • All three recognized species of elephant are endangered.
  • Elephants are considered as intelligent as some primates and cetaceans and appear to be self-aware.
  • Elephants are frequently hunted for their ivory tusks.
